Ngun's Five Course Meal

One-bite hors d’oeuvres: Mini Caprese Skewers

Cherry tomatoes, fresh basil leaves, and mozzarella balls skewered together and drizzled with a balsamic reduction.

Plated appetizer: Lobster Bisque

A creamy and flavorful soup made from lobster stock, heavy cream, and a splash of sherry.

Palate-cleansing salad: Citrus Fennel Salad

Thinly sliced fennel, orange segments, and mixed greens tossed in a light citrus vinaigrette.

The main entrée: Beef Wellington

A tender filet mignon coated in mustard and wrapped in puff pastry. Served with roasted asparagus and a red wine reduction.

Dessert: Tiramisu

A classic Italian dessert made with ladyfingers soaked in coffee and layered with a whipped mixture of eggs, sugar, and mascarpone cheese. Topped with a dusting of cocoa powder.
